Who is Steve Wozniak?

Introduction

Steve Wozniak, also known as "Woz," is a name that rings loud in the halls of technology and innovation. Born on August 11, 1950, in San Jose, California, Wozniak's journey from a curious child with a passion for electronics to a co-founder of Apple Inc. is nothing short of remarkable. His story isn't just about creating one of the most successful companies in the world; it's about a deep love for computing and a drive to make technology accessible to everyone.
