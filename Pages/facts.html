Steve Wozniak - Personal Life

Facts about Steve Wozniak

A True Prankster

Before founding Apple, Steve Wozniak was known for his love of pranks. One of his most notable pranks was during his time at Homestead High School, where he and Steve Jobs attempted to unfurl a giant banner with a provocative gesture during graduation ceremonies. (Source)

Self-Taught Computer Whiz

Wozniak built his first computer, the Apple I, as a side project while working at Hewlett-Packard. His groundbreaking design was initially rejected by HP five times before he and Jobs decided to sell it themselves, leading to the formation of Apple Computer Company. (Source)

A Patron of Music and Festivals

Demonstrating his diverse interests, Wozniak once organized musical festivals during 1982-1983, which were a blend of technology, music, and community. These events were indicative of his passion for bringing people together and his love for music and entertainment. (Source)
