Steve Wozniak - Personal Life

Aviation and Recovery from Accident

Wozniak's interests also include aviation. However, in 1981, he experienced a serious plane crash that resulted in temporary traumatic amnesia, necessitating a sabbatical from Apple. This event led him to temporarily return to the University of California, Berkeley, to complete his degree, showcasing his resilience and determination to overcome personal setbacks. (Source)

Organizing Musical Events

Demonstrating his diverse interests, Wozniak once organized musical festivals during 1982-1983, which were a blend of technology, music, and community. These events were indicative of his passion for bringing people together and his love for music and entertainment. (Source)

Appearances in Media

In addition to his technical and educational pursuits, Wozniak has appeared in several films and TV shows, showcasing his willingness to engage with popular culture and share his story with a broader audience. (Source)
